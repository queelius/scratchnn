## 1. Goal

Draft Chapter 8, "Reinforcement Learning: A Scalar Reward over Trajectories." It changes the learning signal one last time: the per-example label disappears and reappears as a scalar reward spread over a whole trajectory, so the hard new problem is temporal credit assignment. The chapter derives REINFORCE, reads it back as return-weighted softmax cross-entropy (the supervised toolkit from Chapter 1 is literally RL's inner loop), runs a real NumPy gridworld, names AIXI as the incomputable north star (mirroring Chapter 4's Solomonoff), and then closes the whole book by recapping the three axes across all eight chapters.
